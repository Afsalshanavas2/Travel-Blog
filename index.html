How Travel Agency Work

A travel agency works by facilitating travel arrangements for clients. They offer expertise in planning, booking flights, accommodations, tours, and activities. Agents use their knowledge to create personalized itineraries, secure deals, and handle logistics. Their goal is to make travel convenient, enjoyable, and memorable while saving clients time and effort.
